Death Bells Debut EP Now Available For Preorder

by BC

Posted on April 20, 2016 at 9:00 AM

Since forming in 2014, Sydney Australia’s Death Bells have self-released two singles; “You, Me & Everyone In Between” and “Come On, Feel The Come Down”.

After playing shows along the East Coast of Australia with such bands as Gang of Youth, Gold Class, and a main stage slot a Volumes Festival, Death Bells locked themselves away for the summer to write and record their debut EP. While taking influence from bands such as the Go-Betweens, The Chills, and The Saints; Death Bells also experiment with analog drum machines, synthesizers, and string arrangements to create something completely unique and refreshing.

Recorded at the band’s residence and mastered by Total Control’s Mikey Young, their debut self titled EP straddles the legacy of the cold European industrial songwriting of the late 20th Century, whilst paying homage to the sincerity of Australiana. Each song on the record reflects an individual in the band’s experience of growing up; dealing with loss or moving on.

Limited to 200 copies on Black Vinyl and 100 copies on White Vinyl.
